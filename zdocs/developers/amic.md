The AMIC is the I/O controller used in the Power Mac 6100.

It also:

* Controls the video timing signals

## Subdevices

| Subdevice      | Range                |
|:--------------:|:--------------------:|
| VIA Cuda       | 0x0 - 0x1FFF         |
| SCC            | 0x4000 - 0x5FFF      |
| SCSI           | 0x10000 - 0x11FFF    |
| DMA            | 0x31000 - 0x32FFF    |
